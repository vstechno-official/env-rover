## 💡 The Origin

On **14th May 2026**, while travelling by road from Konkan to Mumbai, I noticed plastic bottles, bags, and waste scattered along roadsides and near dumps. That observation sparked a question — *what if autonomous vehicles could clean this up?*

That question became **ENV-ROVER 1.0**.

***

## 🎯 Motive

> Maximize environmental sanitation efficiency by automating the **identification**, **collection**, and **safe disposal** of pollutants — through incineration or government-verified green disposal methods.

India generates millions of tonnes of solid waste annually, much of it ending up on roadsides and in unmanaged dumps. ENV-ROVER aims to flip that equation by taking humans out of the most hazardous parts of the process and putting smart, solar-powered machines in.

***

## 🤖 What is ENV-ROVER?

**ENV-ROVER 1.0** is an autonomous environmental maintenance rover designed for **waste accumulation mitigation across varied terrains**. It is capable of navigating rough, uneven ground — identifying, picking up, and bagging waste — then autonomously returning to a deployment truck once its mission is complete.

Think of it as a Mars rover, but built to clean up Earth.

> Inspired by:
> - 🚀 NASA's Perseverance Rover (Mars 2020 Mission)
> - 🚗 Remote-controlled monster trucks
> - 🤖 OpenAI's Swarm Project
