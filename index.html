The Motive

To maximize environmental sanitation efficiency by automating theidentification, collection, and safe disposal of pollutants throughincineration or govt-verified green disposal methods.

eco

Inception of env-rover idea

On 14th May 2026, I was travelling by road from my native place(Konkan) to Mumbai. On the way i observed plastic bags, bottles and waste along the roads and dumps. This induced a idea if we could use autonomous vehicles to clean road-side waste and dumps.

The Technology

1) solar_power

Solar Panels

High-efficiency photovoltaic array for extended autonomous runtime in remote locations.

2) precision_manufacturing

Collecting Grip

Industrial-grade articulated arm designed for multi-material sorting and secure pickup.

3) tire_repair

Hard Terrain Tyres

Suspended high-traction rubber for navigation across mud, sand, and rubble.

4) sprinkler

Disinfectant Sprayer

Integrated chemical dispersal unit for post-collection site sanitation.

location_on
Location Deployment & Pickup

Point-to-point GPS navigation allowing for remote release and automated recovery after operational windows.
